Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Applies to all Vexora Studio apps

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, we'll make it right. No complicated process.

1. Free Trial

All Pro subscriptions include a 3-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial. Cancel before the trial ends and you will not be charged at all.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 days of your first payment
  • You experienced a technical issue we were unable to resolve
  • You were charged incorrectly or charged after cancelling

3. How to Request a Refund

Email support@vexorastudio.com with:

  • Your account email address
  • The date of purchase
  • The reason for your refund request

We process refunds within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

We generally do not offer refunds for:

  • Requests made more than 7 days after the charge
  • Renewal charges where you forgot to cancel beforehand
  • Partial months or unused subscription time beyond the 7-day window

However, we review every request individually. If you have a genuine concern, reach out — we'll do our best to help.

5. Subscription Cancellation

Cancelling your subscription stops future charges but does not automatically generate a refund for the current period. You keep Pro access until the period ends. Cancel from the Profile tab in the app or by emailing support@vexorastudio.com.

6. Contact

Email support@vexorastudio.com — we respond within 24 hours.
