Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Applies to all Vexora Studio apps

We built Abide and all Vexora Studio apps with your privacy in mind. We collect only what we need to make the app work, and we never sell your data to anyone.

1. Who We Are

Vexora Studio is an independent app developer. We build mobile applications including Abide — Daily Prayer & Bible. You can contact us at privacy@vexorastudio.com.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address and name when you create an account
  • Usage data: Streak count, completed tasks, selected prayer guides, and app preferences
  • Prayer journal entries: Text you write in the journal (stored in your account)
  • AI conversation history: Messages to AI Father (stored locally on your device only)
  • Payment information: We do not store payment details — processed by Paddle
  • Device information: Basic device type and OS for compatibility purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our apps and services
  • Sync your progress and preferences across devices
  • Send you prayer reminders (only if you enable them)
  • Process subscription payments via Paddle
  • Respond to support requests
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse

4. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase with industry-standard security. AI Father conversation history is stored only on your device and never sent to our servers. You can clear it anytime from within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Paddle — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • Groq — AI language model for AI Father (privacy policy)
  • Bible API — public domain Bible text, no personal data shared

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data with the third-party services listed above, as necessary to provide our services.

7. Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where legally required to retain it.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Contact us at privacy@vexorastudio.com to exercise any of these rights.

9. Account Deletion

Delete your account from the Profile tab in the app, or email privacy@vexorastudio.com. We will delete your account within 7 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance. We will notify users of significant changes.

12. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@vexorastudio.com
