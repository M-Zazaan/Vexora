Abide App
What is Abide? +

Abide is a spiritual wellness app for daily prayer, guided Bible reading, personal journaling, and AI-powered reflection. It's designed to help you build a consistent, meaningful prayer habit.

What is AI Father? +

AI Father is a spiritually-grounded AI companion powered by Groq. It provides guided reflection, encouragement, and prayer prompts. Conversations are stored only on your device and never sent to our servers. It's not a substitute for professional pastoral counseling.

Does Abide work offline? +

Yes — Pro subscribers have full offline access to the Bible and all prayer guides. Your journal also syncs when you're back online.

Which Bible translation does Abide use? +

Abide uses the World English Bible (WEB), a public domain translation. Additional translations may be added in future updates.

Can I use Abide without creating an account? +

Basic features are available without an account, but you'll need to sign up to sync your journal, track streaks across devices, and access Pro features.

Billing & Subscriptions
How does the free trial work? +

All Pro subscriptions include a 3-day free trial. You won't be charged during the trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged at all.

How much does Pro cost? +

Abide Pro is $4.99/month or $41.99/year (saving ~30%). Check our pricing page for the latest pricing.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

Cancel from the Profile tab in the app, or email support@vexorastudio.com. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund? +

Yes — we offer a 7-day refund policy on your first payment. See our Refund Policy for full details, or email support@vexorastudio.com.

Who processes payments? +

Payments are processed securely by Paddle. We never store your payment details on our servers.

Privacy & Data
What data do you collect? +

We collect only what's needed to run the app: your email, usage data (streaks, completed tasks), and journal entries. We never sell your data. See our full Privacy Policy.

Are my journal entries private? +

Yes. Journal entries are stored securely in your account and never shared with third parties. Only you can access them.

How do I delete my account? +

Delete your account from the Profile tab in the app, or email privacy@vexorastudio.com. We'll delete your data within 7 days.
